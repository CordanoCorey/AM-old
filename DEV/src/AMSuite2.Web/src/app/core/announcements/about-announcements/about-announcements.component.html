About Announcements: Use an announcement to alert your users of information you want your organization to see.

There are 3 types of announcements:

System: System wide announcements for things like scheduled down times, new features etc.

Account: Announcements to a particular account composed by an account administrator for that account.

Group: Announcements to a particular group, composed by a group administrator for that group.

Announcements appear on a users Dashboard once they are logged in. You can control when and how long an annoucement is displayed by supplying a begin and end date on this form.
